## The 6-Phase Compliance Journey

Veritome organises your work into six clear phases, mirroring the EU AI Act's own structure. Each phase unlocks after the previous one is complete, so you always know exactly where you are.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="1. Classify" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Assign a risk tier, role, and Annex III domain. Article 5 prohibited-practice screening runs first.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2. Scope & Literacy" icon="graduation-cap">
    Complete Art. 4 staff literacy, confirm instructions-for-use receipt, and finalise scope.
  </Card>
  <Card title="3. Build & Document" icon="file-pen">
    Work through Risk Management (Art. 9), Data Governance (Art. 10), and Human Oversight (Art. 14).
  </Card>
  <Card title="4. Conformity" icon="badge-check">
    Run your FRIA (Art. 27), choose a conformity route (Art. 43), and prepare CE readiness.
  </Card>
  <Card title="5. Register" icon="clipboard-list">
    Export your EU database / Annex VIII registration (Art. 49) and go live.
  </Card>
  <Card title="6. Operate & Monitor" icon="chart-line">
    Stay compliant with Transparency (Art. 50), post-market monitoring (Art. 72), and incident reporting (Art. 73).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Get Started in Minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [veritome.eu](https://veritome.eu) — the Free plan gives you one AI system with no credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register your first AI system">
    From your dashboard, click **New System** and enter a name and short description. Veritome's guided wizard takes it from there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the classification wizard">
    Answer a series of branching questions to determine your system's risk tier, role, and applicable Annex III domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your obligations">
    Veritome instantly maps the exact EU AI Act obligations that apply to your system and opens a compliance Kanban board.
  </Step>
</Steps>
