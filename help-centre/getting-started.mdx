This guide walks you through the essentials of getting up and running with Veritome.

## 1. Create your account

Sign up at [veritome.com](https://veritome.com) and verify your email address.

## 2. Set up your workspace

Invite team members, configure your organization profile, and choose your plan.

## 3. Run your first assessment

Follow the guided flow to classify your first AI system under the EU AI Act.
