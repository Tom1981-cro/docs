## Account & billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade or change my plan?">
    You can manage your subscription from the billing section in your workspace settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I add team members?">
    Go to workspace settings and invite users by email. Roles and permissions can be configured per user.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using Veritome

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does Veritome classify AI systems?">
    Veritome uses the EU AI Act framework to guide you through risk classification.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my assessments?">
    Yes — assessments can be exported as PDF or structured data from the dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
