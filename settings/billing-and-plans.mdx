## Annual Billing Discount

Switch to annual billing to pay for 10 months and get 12 — a **20% saving** compared to the monthly rate. Annual billing is charged as a single upfront payment and renews automatically unless cancelled.

| Plan | Monthly billing | Annual billing (per month) | Annual saving |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | €59/mo | €47.20/mo | €141.60/yr |
| Core | €149/mo | €119.20/mo | €357.60/yr |
| Growth | €349/mo | €279.20/mo | €837.60/yr |
| Business | €699/mo | €559.20/mo | €1,677.60/yr |

To switch to annual billing:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Switch to annual billing**.
3. Review the prorated amount and click **Confirm** in the Stripe portal.

## Founding Member Discount

Veritome offers a **founding member discount of 30% off annual billing for 3 years**, available to the first 100 organisations to activate it.

<Tip>
  The founding member discount stacks on top of the annual billing discount — you pay for annual billing at 30% off the monthly price for three consecutive annual billing cycles. After three years, your subscription reverts to the standard annual rate.
</Tip>

To check whether founding member pricing is still available and to apply it:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Available discounts**.
2. If founding member pricing is still available, a banner shows the remaining spots. Click **Claim founding member discount**.
3. The discount is applied to your next billing cycle if you are already on annual billing, or immediately if you switch to annual billing at the same time.

<Warning>
  The founding member discount is non-transferable and applies to the organisation account that claims it, not to individual users. It cannot be applied retroactively to previous billing periods.
</Warning>

## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Billing">
    Click **Settings** in the left sidebar, then select **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Change Plan">
    Under the **Current plan** section, click **Change plan**. A plan picker shows all available plans with your current plan highlighted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your new plan">
    Click the plan you want to upgrade to. A pricing summary shows the prorated amount due today (for upgrades mid-cycle) or at next renewal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout in Stripe">
    Click **Upgrade now**. You are taken to the Stripe billing portal to confirm the payment method and complete the transaction. Once confirmed, your new plan is active immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Upgrades take effect immediately. If you upgrade mid-cycle, Veritome calculates a prorated charge for the remainder of the current billing period, and your next full charge is at the new plan rate.
</Info>

## Downgrading Your Plan

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle — you retain all features of your current plan until the cycle ends.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan">
    Follow the same path as for upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a lower plan">
    Choose the plan you want to move to. Veritome shows a **Downgrade impact** warning listing any features or capacity you will lose (e.g. *"You have 8 systems — Core plan allows 5. You must archive or delete 3 systems before the downgrade takes effect."*).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolve capacity conflicts">
    If your current data exceeds the limits of the target plan (systems, members, etc.), resolve the conflicts before confirming the downgrade. Unresolved conflicts will block the downgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the downgrade">
    Click **Schedule downgrade**. The downgrade is confirmed and scheduled for end of cycle. You receive a confirmation email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading Invoices

Every invoice Veritome issues is available in the Stripe billing portal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Billing → Invoices">
    Click **Billing** in the left sidebar settings, then select the **Invoices** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Open billing portal'">
    Click **Open billing portal**. You are redirected to the Stripe customer portal authenticated as your organisation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download invoices">
    Under **Invoice history**, click the **Download** icon next to any invoice to download a PDF. You can also update billing details and payment methods from the same portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancelling Your Subscription

To cancel your Veritome subscription:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Cancel subscription** at the bottom of the page.
3. You are redirected to the Stripe portal where you confirm cancellation. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.
4. After cancellation, your account moves to the Free plan limits. Systems and data above Free plan limits are read-only until you either resubscribe or reduce your usage to Free plan limits.

<Accordion title="Data retention after cancellation">
  Veritome retains all your organisation's data (systems, dossiers, evidence, reports) for 90 days after a subscription lapses. During this window you can resubscribe and resume without data loss. After 90 days, data is permanently deleted in accordance with Veritome's data retention policy. Contact support@veritome.eu before the 90-day window expires if you need an extension or a data export.
</Accordion>

## Enterprise Plan

The Enterprise plan is for organisations with complex requirements: large AI system portfolios, custom contractual SLAs, dedicated implementation support, or specific data residency needs.

Enterprise pricing is custom and negotiated directly. To discuss an Enterprise plan:

1. Visit [veritome.eu/enterprise](https://veritome.eu/enterprise) or click **Contact sales** on the Billing page.
2. A member of the Veritome sales team will reach out within one business day.
