Whether you have a question about your compliance programme, need help with the platform, or want to speak with someone about whether Veritome is right for your organisation, there are several ways to get in touch. This page covers every support channel, response time by plan, and additional resources that may answer your question before you need to reach out.

<Note>
  Veritome is a compliance management platform, not a law firm. We can help you understand and implement EU AI Act obligations using our platform — but we cannot provide legal advice. For questions about how the regulation applies to your specific legal situation, consult a qualified EU law practitioner.
</Note>

## Support channels

### Contact form and email

The primary way to reach the Veritome support team is through the contact form at [veritome.eu/contact](https://veritome.eu/contact). You can also email us directly using the address provided on that page.

When contacting support about a technical issue, please include:
- Your organisation name and the email address associated with your account
- The name or ID of the AI system involved (if applicable)
- A clear description of the issue and the steps that led to it
- Any error messages shown in the platform
- Screenshots if the issue is visual

The more detail you provide upfront, the faster we can diagnose and resolve your issue.

## Book a demo

If you are evaluating Veritome for your organisation — or if you want a guided walkthrough of how the platform handles your specific AI systems and compliance scenario — you can book a 30-minute demo with the Veritome team.

Demos are live, personalised sessions (not a recorded product tour). Bring your questions about your AI systems, your compliance timeline, and how Veritome fits into your existing workflows.

**Book a demo:** [veritome.eu/book](https://veritome.eu/book)

## Free readiness assessment

Before booking a demo or starting a paid plan, consider taking the free readiness assessment. It walks you through the key EU AI Act questions in under ten minutes and gives you:

- A risk-tier indication for your AI systems
- A summary of the obligations that are likely to apply
- A compliance gap report showing where your biggest priorities are

No account is required and there is no commitment. It is the fastest way to understand where you stand.

**Start the assessment:** [veritome.eu/apps/readiness-assessment](https://veritome.eu/apps/readiness-assessment)

## Trust Center

The Veritome Trust Center contains everything you need to evaluate Veritome's security posture, data handling practices, and compliance with applicable laws — including:

- Data residency information (all customer data stored in the EEA)
- Subprocessor list
- Security certifications and audit reports
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
- Outbound IP ranges (for webhook and integration firewall rules)
- Penetration test summaries
- Incident response policy

**Visit the Trust Center:** [veritome.eu/trust](https://veritome.eu/trust)
